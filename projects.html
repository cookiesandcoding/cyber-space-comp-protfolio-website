IEEE Club Expense Tracker App

Github-link : Click here

As a key member of the backend development team for the IEEE Club Expense Tracker App, I actively contributed by creating 5-6 pull requests (PRs) aimed at enhancing backend functionalities and ensuring robust performance.

Manage and monitor all expenses seamlessly with the IEEE Club Expense Tracker App, designed to streamline financial management for all club activities and events. Whether it's overseeing event costs, tracking reimbursements, or monitoring budget allocations, this app provides a comprehensive solution tailored to the needs of IEEE club members.

Key Features:

  • Event Expense Management: Easily record and categorize expenses related to all club events, including workshops, seminars, and social gatherings.
  • Real-time Tracking: Monitor expenses in real-time to stay within budget limits and ensure financial transparency.
  • Receipt Uploads: Capture and upload receipts directly through the app to simplify reimbursement processes and maintain accurate records.
  • Budget Allocation: Plan and allocate budgets for different events and activities, ensuring efficient use of club funds.
  • Multi-user Access: Enable authorized club members to access financial data securely, facilitating collaboration and decision-making.
  • Financial Reports: Generate detailed reports on expenditures, allowing for thorough analysis and future planning.
